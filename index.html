About Us

Welcome to Adventure Amazon! Adventure Amazon is a Travel Agency providing unique tours to Manu Rainforest in Peru. Our goal is to provide an exciting adventure into the Amazon Rainforest and to deliver our guests a satisfying, unique experience. Our guides, drivers and cooks are all highly trained conservationists, striving to protect our beautiful rainforests. Our quest begins and ends in the historical town of Cusco, Peru. We take you over the Andes, into the Amazon and back again. Come with us for an unforgettable experience into the Amazon!

About Manu

Manu is one of the most bio-diverse regions in the World. The bio-reserve is almost exclusively accessed through Cusco, Peru. With 15,000 species of plants and over 1000 species of birds, Manu provides you with an experience you can find nowhere else on Earth. Mammals, Reptiles, Amphibians, Fish, and Insects fill the region. On our journey you will travel down the world-famous Manu Road. Every 50 meters of your descent hosts its own unique ecosystem and species. When we reach the river, you will begin your adventure into Amazon Wildlife. If diversity is what you seek, Manu is for you!

4 Day Trip (Cultural Zone)

Day 1: Cusco → Bamboo Lodge

  • Departure from Hotel in Cusco
  • Breakfast in Huancarani
  • Ninamarka Archeological Site
  • Paucartambo Museum
  • Manu National Park
  • Wildlife Spotting in the Cloud Forest
  • Lunch on Manu Roa
  • Wildlife Spotting on Manu Road
  • Dinner upon arrival at Bamboo Lodge

Our trip begins around 6:00 am from your Hotel. Our guide will pick you up, along with other guests on your trip. We will start the trip by driving up the beautiful Andes Mountains. After a quick breakfast in the small town of Hauncarani, we will visit the pre-Inca Archeological site of Ninamarka and the Paucartambo Inca Museum. After we cross the peaks, we will start our decent into the Cloud Forest. This is the entrance to the Amazon Rainforest and will be our first chance to see unique flora and fauna, including monkeys, unique plants, and several bird species. If we are lucky, we might see Peru's national bird, "Cock of the Rock." We will stop for lunch down this legendry road, spotting wildlife all along the way. When we reach the bottom, we will arrive at the "Bamboo Lodge" for the night. Here we will enjoy dinner and have a look around the forest.

Day 2: Bamboo lodge → Soga de

  • Morning walk around the Lodge
  • Breakfast
  • Visit to Wildlife Recue Center
  • Wildlife Spotting on the way to Atalaya
  • Motorized Canoe to Soga de Oro Lodge
  • Lunch
  • Rainforest Trek
  • Dinner
  • Optional Night Hike

In the morning, we will take a small walk around the lodge to see the local banana trees and crops. Then we will enjoy breakfast before starting our drive towards Atalaya. We will spot many species on the road. We will also stop at a small Wildlife Rescue Center, where several animals have been rescued from the Black Market. When we reach Atalaya, we will get our Rubber Boots and transfer to a motorized canoe. This will take us down the Rio Madre de Dios to the Soga de Oro Lodge where we will be spending the next two nights. We will have lunch and trek around the surrounding rainforest. After dinner, we will offer an optional night hike to see nocturnal animals like snakes, frogs, and insects.
